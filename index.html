Roses Are Red

Rosa rubiginosa is the scientific name for rose. Roses belong to the Rosaceae family, and there are about 360 species in the genus Rosa. Erect bushes with stems are their defining characteristics. Roses come in a variety of colours, such as red, white, yellow, blue, and many others
